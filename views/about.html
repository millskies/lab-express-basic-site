Pocholo Buldin is a portuguese hound mix
born in May 12th of 2017. Pocholo is very
well known for barking at people in the street
and for loving the chicken that his 'belito' gives
him. Also, Pocholo has won several trophies on
"Best nap taker" for 6 consecutive years.

His carrer started when he was adopted by his
beautiful and awesome mom, who showed him
the art of napping. With time and practice Pocholo
became a full expert at this topic, surprising everyone
(including his beautiful and awesome mom) with his
strange nap positions.
